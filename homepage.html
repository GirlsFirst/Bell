Welcome to Bell! Our team has collected helpful resources and information to create an easy environment for you to navigate through. Our website is targeted at providing reminders and resources to the elderly. Here, you can monitor and schedule your appointments and prescriptions. You can get involved in your community and connect with others all with a few clicks!



About Us

Bell was created to help the elderly and make it easier for them to get involved in their community, along with keeping track of their daily needs. By reminding them to take their medications, providing them with our “Handy Helpers”, and supplying the dates and information needed to get involved in their communities, we see significant improvement in their lives for the long run.
